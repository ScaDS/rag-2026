# Known Parallel Codes

## List of Known Parallel Codes at ZIH HPC

With the introduction of new [NHR](https://www.nhr-verein.de/) templates for the detailed project
description of NHR Normal and Large HPC projects on 1st Oct 2025, it is mandatory to include a
scaling analysis for the proposed simulation codes.
An exception is made for those programs and software that are known to run efficiently in
parallel on our system. In the following, we provide a list of codes which do not require a
scaling analysis to be conducted within your proposal.

!!! abstract "FEM"

    - ABAQUS
    - ANSYS
    - COMSOL
    - LS-DYNA

!!! abstract "Life Science"

    - FastQC
    - FreeSurfer
    - FSL

!!! abstract "Earth and Climate Science"

    - PALM
    - WRF

!!! abstract "Nanoscale Simulation"
